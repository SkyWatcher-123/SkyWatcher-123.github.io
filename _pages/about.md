Jiaying (Vina) Wei is a third year Ph.D. candidate in Computational Design. Her research focuses on parametric robotic fabrication, online robot planning, vision and learning in the construction domain. She has a background in digital assembly and casting fabrication. Vina obtained her B.Arch degree at Cornell University and Master of Science in Computational Design at Carnegie Mellon.
